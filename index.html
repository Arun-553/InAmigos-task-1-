Who we are

An NGO built on one simple idea — nobody should be left behind

InAmigos Foundation was founded on 23 September 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla, with a single goal: to bring unity and support back into a society that often feels divided. What started as a small group of volunteers in Chhattisgarh has grown into a movement working across education, food security, women's empowerment, animal welfare and the environment.

We focus on the gaps that usually get overlooked — children who drop out of school because of poverty, families that go hungry, women with no path to financial independence, stray animals with nowhere to go, and neighbourhoods losing their green cover. Every InAmigos project is built to close one of these gaps.

Our Projects

Six causes, one mission

Every project targets a specific gap in society. Together, they form a complete ecosystem of care.

Project Seva

Food & clothing drives

Regular distribution of food and clothing to underprivileged and homeless communities, making sure no one sleeps hungry.

Project Bachpanshala

Education for children

Quality learning support for children from low-income families, so poverty doesn't decide who gets to go to school.

Project Jeev

Animal welfare

Rescue, protection and feeding programs for stray and at-risk animals across our operating regions.

Project Udaan

Women's empowerment

Skill development and livelihood programs that help women build financial independence on their own terms.

Project Prakriti

Environment & sustainability

Tree plantation and conservation drives aimed at restoring green cover and improving environmental quality.

Project Vikas

Skill & internship training

Employability training that has reached over 30,000 interns, building real digital and professional skills.
